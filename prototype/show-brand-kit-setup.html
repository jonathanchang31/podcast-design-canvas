Guardrails

Defaults stay available Creators can keep working with strong defaults even when brand inputs are incomplete.
Conflicts stay previewable A flagged brand choice stays visible on real episode content so the creator can judge the trade-off before saving it.
No silent fixes Readability adjustments should be suggested or confirmed, never applied invisibly behind the creator’s back.

Connected workflow

  • brand inputs preview across preset cards, captions, lower-thirds, and thumbnails
  • readability conflicts stay separate from unrelated caption or sponsor warnings
  • accepted inputs carry forward through show template adaptation
  • already exported episodes do not get rewritten when the template changes later
